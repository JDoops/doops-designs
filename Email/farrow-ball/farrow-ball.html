New Latest & Greatest Wallpaper Collection
1st‌ September‌ 2016‌ will see the launch of our new Latest & Greatest collection. As Farrow & Ball celebrates its 70th birthday this year the new collection of wallpapers has taken inspiration from the decade in which the company was founded. Embracing the paradoxical feel of the post-war period, a time of looking forward while stepping back, the three uniquely textured papers are contemporary interpretations of the classic 1940’s prints.

The collection consists of three new wallpaper designs each available in seven colourways ranging from delicate neutrals and muted pastels, to bolder tones with metallic accents.

If you hold our six wallpaper books you will receive a launch pack in July detailing the new collection and the marketing and merchandising items, you will receive to support the launch of these new papers. You can access a digital copy of the brief and order form for displays rolls by clicking the link below.

To make room for the new wallpaper collection in your Latest & Greatest Wallpaper Book, we have archived wallpapers from across the entire collection.

Please refer to the Wallpaper Movement & Archiving document contained within your launch pack which details those papers due to be archived and those that will move from the Latest & Greatest book into the core range. We ask that you use the information in the Wallpaper Movement & Archiving document and work with your sales representative / field agent to ensure your books are updated and current by 31st‌ August‌ 2016‌.

The newly archived papers will be subject to a three roll minimum order and longer delivery time of 14 working days from 1st‌ September‌ 2016‌.

A4 samples of these papers will only be available until 31st‌ October‌ 2016‌.

However, Farrow & Ball will waive the newly archived paper three roll minimum until 31st‌ December‌ 2016‌ to allow for decorating decisions being made prior to September‌.

2016 Stockist Conference and Awards Night
On Wednesday‌ 15th‌ and Thursday‌ 16th‌ June‌ we hosted our Annual New Stockist Conference. We were thrilled to have over 100 stockists from around the world joining us for two days of presentations, colour talks, technical training and a site tour including sessions in the paint and wallpaper factory.

On the Thursday‌ night‌, we held the third Farrow & Ball Stockist Awards Night with over 140 guests attending and four stockists being presented with awards. Overall, the Conference was a huge success and we have already received a generous amount of positive feedback! Thank you to all who attended.
 
 
 
 
Our 2016 awards winners were:
 
• Best Newcomer: Huddersfield ‌Decorating Merchants, UK‌
 
• EU Stockist of the Year: Le Decorazioni, IT‌
 
• NA Stockist of the Year: Rings End, USA‌
 
• UK & IE Stockist of the Year: Rabart Decorating Centres, UK‌
 
You can read more about these winners in this month’s Stockist Focus section. There was a very high standard of entries for this year’s event which made the decision making process all the more challenging.

The Farrow & Ball team would like to say a big thank you to all our stockists for their drive, creativity and support of the Farrow & Ball brand. We wouldn’t be here without you!

STOCKIST FOCUS
 
This update we are focusing on the winners of the 2016 stockist awards.

Best Newcomer Winner – Huddersfield‌ Decorators Merchants, UK‌
We missed Rob and the team at the event but their sales representative Kirsty Ardis was on hand to collect the award in their absence.

Huddersfield Decorator’s Merchants are a family owned business, set up in 1990 by Rob Kimberlin. They became Farrow & Ball stockists in January‌ 2015.

The team are all members of the Kimberlin family and have a wealth of knowledge and expertise around paint, wallpaper and interior design. Even though they know almost everything there is to know about paint, they embraced Farrow & Ball Training and were delighted to see how we could help them to develop their business.

Rob and his team have good contacts with local tradesmen and many of his regular customers have asked about visiting F&B and how they can work more closely with us. As a Farrow & Ball stockist, they have also seen their traditional customer base expand, to include many more retail customers.
 
 
 
 
Runners up for this category were:
 
• Bach Johansen, Norway‌
 
• Reeds, UK‌
 
EU Stockist of the Year Winner – Le Decorazioni, Rome‌, UK‌
Stefano della Corte and his wife Giorgia started working with F&B as a stockist in Rome in 2008. Since then they have worked hard to grow local brand awareness through numerous events. They have developed a significant market for F&B and sales have grown rapidly year on year.

The store offers a full application service which is immensely popular with customers. They have also established excellent contacts with interior designers and worked on many prestigious projects including with the Vatican itself.
 
 
 
 
Runners up for this category were:
 
• Fessler Kamine, Austria‌
 
• Breed Interieur, Holland‌
 
NA Stockist of the Year Winner – Rings End‌, NY‌ & CT‌, USA‌
Ring's End is one of CT’s most prominent retail lumber, millwork, and building supply companies, with 17 stores across NY and CT. In business since 1902, they have become the authority in home building and renovation for homeowners, contractors, and designers alike. Known for their excellence in service and an unmatched selection of product, Ring’s End is the perfect partner for growth in the Connecticut market.

In the past year, they have increased their F&B distribution from 2 locations to 5 locations, adding Danbury‌, Wilton‌, and Branford‌, trailing our new retail display unit. They also teamed with us on the "CT Uplift", a trial program for local advertising across channels whereby we collaborate between showroom and stockist to elevate an entire market.

If that weren’t enough, they also partnered with us on a number of successful events, not the least of which was hosting the CT leg of our NA Painter's Tour at their Stratford training facility. All around, a fantastic partner and one that we will surely grow further with in the year to come.
 
 
 
 
Runners up for this category were:
 
• Colors by Martin – NY‌, USA‌
 
• Johnson Paint – Boston‌, USA‌
 
UK & IE Stockist of the Year Winner – Rabart Decorating Centres, Wales & South West‌, UK‌
Rabarts is a family owned Decorators Merchant and long standing stockist, with eight branches throughout Southern Wales and South West England. During 2015, they invested significantly in Farrow & Ball throughout each of these branches, with large colour walls, new custom shelving and enhanced Farrow & Ball point of sale materials at the heart of each refurbishment. Key to their success is the extensive stock holding in each branch, with up to 15 metres of floor space being dedicated to Farrow & Ball. Rabarts really understand the importance of being able to fulfil customers’ requirements whilst visiting their stores.

They have recruited a dedicated colour consultant and decorative assistant for each branch which has improved customer service and translated into increased sales. Every member of the Rabarts team recognises the value of the Farrow & Ball brand and product. The efforts and investments made have paid off, with an impressive growth of +37% in the last year.
 
 
 
 
Runners up for this category were:
 
• Romerils, Jersey, UK‌
 
• Ashby Decorating Centre, Leicestershire‌, UK‌
