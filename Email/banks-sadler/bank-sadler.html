ETC: County Hall opens 2017
This will be located opposite the Houses of Parliament and will be ETC venues largest venue yet offering 20 meeting spaces. This will be complimented by two events spaces catering for up to 900 guests with uninterrupted views of the Houses of Parliament and Big Ben.
